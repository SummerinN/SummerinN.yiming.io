About Me

I am an information designer and a fresh master's graduated from design for data visualization in UAL. My research revolves around making datasets and complex information systems to be more experiential and legible with design and exploring relationships among behavioral psychology, game design, culture, and narratology. Recent projects focused on the impact of algorithms filtering information, like explaining the public perception of AI techniques under media exposure, extracting the features of information provided by generated AI on daily decisions , and symbols in the fields of games, human-computer interaction, etc. In Oct 2024, a collaboration project between the team I was in and IMF was revealed in Washington D.C. I have an internship designing data dashboard for digital service company, and a vast part-time working experience in video creation, media content design and graphic design. During my undergraduate study in digital media design at UIC, I also had two experiences creating graphics, art, and narration for game development teams.
